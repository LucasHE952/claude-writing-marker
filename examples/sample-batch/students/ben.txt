I disagree that schools should require uniforms. I think students should be allowed to wear what they want.

One reason is that clothes is a way to show who you are. Every person has different style. Some students like sports clothes, some like color, some like simple. If everyone wear the same uniform, you cannot see this difference and the school feel boring.

Also, uniforms can be uncomfortable. The shirts are sometimes too hot in summer, and the pants can be too tight. When students are uncomfortable, they cannot concentrate well in class. I know this because at my old school the uniform was very thick polyester and we sweated a lot in May.

Another reason is that uniforms are expensive. Parents have to buy two or three sets, and they get dirty quickly. Normal clothes can be cheaper if you shop carefully, and you can use them outside school too.

So in conclusion, uniforms are not a good idea. Let students choose their own clothes. Then they will be happier and more comfortable.
