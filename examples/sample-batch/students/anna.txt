Should schools require uniforms? In my opinion, schools should require uniforms because it helps students focus on learning instead of clothes.

The first reason is that uniforms save time in the morning. Many students spend a long time choosing what to wear. For example, my cousin always wake up early because she need to choose her clothes. If she has uniform, she can sleep more and arrive on time.

The second reason is that uniforms make all students look the same. This is important because some families have less money and cannot buy expensive clothes. When everyone wears the same thing, no one feels embarassed about their clothes.

Some people might say that uniforms take away students freedom. They think students should express themselves through fashion. However, students can express themselves in many other ways, like through their art, their hobbies, or how they speak. Clothes is not the only way.

In conclusion, I believe uniforms are a good idea for schools. They save time and make everyone feel equal. Schools should keep this rule.
